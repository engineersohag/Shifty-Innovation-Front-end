Service Policy

Scope of Services

At Shifty Innovation, we provide end-to-end digital solutions tailored to your business needs. Our services include:

  • UI & UX Design: We craft user-centric, intuitive, and visually appealing interfaces that enhance user satisfaction and engagement.
  • Mobile App Development: From concept to deployment, we build responsive and performance-driven mobile applications for both Android and iOS platforms.
  • Web Development: Our team develops scalable and secure websites using modern technologies, tailored to meet your goals and branding.
  • No-Code Development: For fast-track solutions, we utilize no-code platforms (e.g., Webflow, Bubble) to create functional apps with minimal development time.
  • IT Outsourcing: We help companies reduce operational costs by offering offshore or remote development teams for long-term or short-term projects.
  • IT Staff Augmentation: We provide skilled professionals to work as an extension of your in-house team, enhancing productivity and speed.

Project Onboarding

Project onboarding begins once the client formally approves the proposal and makes the initial payment. At this stage, we assign a dedicated project manager who is responsible for overseeing the entire process, ensuring effective communication, timely updates, and alignment with project goals. We initiate the onboarding process with a kickoff meeting to establish scope, timelines, communication methods, and expectations on both sides.

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide all required materials and feedback on time
  • Approve deliverables or changes promptly
  • Give access to relevant systems (if needed)

Payment Terms

Our payment structure is flexible and pre-agreed upon, whether fixed-price, hourly, or milestone-based. We issue invoices accordingly, and payment is expected within 7 to 15 business days of the invoice date. Delayed payments may result in project suspension or additional late fees, as outlined in the service agreement. Final deliverables may also be withheld until outstanding payments are cleared.

Revision Policy

Revisions are an important part of the creative process. Each project includes 2 to 3 standard revision cycles at every major stage of delivery. These revisions must be requested within a reasonable period after delivery. Any changes beyond this scope or after final approval will be considered new requests and may incur additional charges based on their complexity.

Confidentiality

Confidentiality is one of our top priorities. All client data, project information, source codes, and designs are kept strictly confidential and shared only with authorized personnel. We do not reuse or disclose any part of the project without the clientâ€™s written permission. If required, we are open to signing a non-disclosure agreement (NDA) to further protect client interests.

Termination Policy

Both the client and Shifty Innovation reserve the right to terminate the agreement at any time with written notice. Upon termination, all work completed up to that point will be invoiced, and payments must be cleared before handover of any deliverables. Any advance payment made is generally non-refundable unless stated otherwise in the initial agreement.

Support & Maintenance

Finally, we provide post-launch support for a limited time to address any bugs or minor issues. For long-term maintenance, platform upgrades, or technical support, clients may opt for a separate maintenance contract tailored to their ongoing needs. Our goal is to build lasting relationships through transparency, reliability, and quality service delivery.

Privacy Policy

At Shifty Innovation, we deeply value your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you engage with our website, services, or communication channels. We believe in complete transparency and assure you that all data is handled responsibly and in accordance with applicable laws and best practices.

Information We Collect

  • Name, company name, email address, and contact number
  • Project-related data and materials shared during collaboration
  • Technical data from website visits (e.g., IP address, browser type)

How We Use Your Information

  • Communicate project updates and proposals
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Manage project logistics and deliverables
  • Send occasional updates or promotional content (with your consent)

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent user data. Information may be shared only with trusted third parties involved in service delivery under strict confidentiality.

Data Security

We use the collected information primarily to communicate with you regarding project proposals, timelines, updates, and deliverables. This information also helps us improve the quality of our services, enhance user experience, and streamline project execution. Occasionally, with your explicit consent, we may send you promotional offers, newsletters, or updates about new services and innovations. You can opt-out of such communication at any time.

We want to assure you that Shifty Innovation does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. Your data may only be shared with our trusted partners, such as subcontractors or service providers who are directly involved in delivering the services you have requested. Even in such cases, strict confidentiality agreements are in place to ensure your information remains secure and private.

Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies and similar technologies to offer you a better browsing experience. Cookies help us understand user behavior anonymously, remember your preferences, and optimize our website content. You have the option to disable cookies via your browser settings, although doing so may impact some features or functionality of the site.
Please note that our website may contain links to third-party websites for your convenience. These external sites have their own privacy policies, and we are not responsible for their content, data collection practices, or security standards. We encourage users to review those policies before interacting with any third-party platform.

Third-Party Links

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party websites linked from our site.

Your Rights

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Correct inaccuracies in your data
  • Request deletion of your data (as per legal requirements)

Policy Updates

Shifty Innovation may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, or legal requirements. Significant changes will be communicated to users via our website or by direct email, ensuring that you remain informed of how your information is handled.
